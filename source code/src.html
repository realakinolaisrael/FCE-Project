Welcome to The Computer Education Research Journal (CERJ)

ISSN: 24656747

The Computer Educators Association of Nigeria (CEAN) is a network of experts in different fields of computer & robotics education and practice with the intent of fostering access to ICT skills and knowledge to students at all levels of Education. The Association was born on 27th March, 2013, when a group of computer educators residing in Nsukka and its environs, gathered and considered it necessary to form a national body to promote computer & robotics education in Nigeria. It is a forum where issues affecting computer & robotics education, applications of computer in education, trends in the methodologies involved in computer & robotics education and best practices are discussed

The Computer Education Research Journal (CERJ) provides a forum for the publication of original research and technical papers, short communications, state-of-the-art computing and review papers on advances, techniques, practice, and applications of Computer Education. The Journal, which is national in coverage, is published once in a year in October.

ABOUT CERJ
The Computer Education Research Journal (CERJ) is intended to provide and disseminate research findings/reports on issues, concepts, and application of computers in promoting/enhancing the knowledge of computing in teaching and learning. Thus, articles which are of high quality and present the results on the integration of computing in current and relevant issues in computer education are welcome for publication in CERJ.
